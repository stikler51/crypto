About Us

Mission statement
We are a company of passionate people creating the perfect cryptocurrency payment solution to give the world unlimited features of blockchain payments in synergy with a traditional system.

Cryptoprocessing is an ecosystem that consists of two solutions: Crypto payment processing for business under Cryptoprocessing brand and a personal wallet for individuals under CoinsPaid brand.

Cryptoprocessing is a brand affiliated with SoftSwiss group. The group consists of 5 brands: SoftSwiss, Merkeleon, Bgaming, Cryptoprocessing and CoinsPaid.

Our history

Cryptoprocessing was launched as an application for Bitcoin payments in 2014.

The ecosystem was created by the developers of 1st provably fair casino and the 1st security audited crypto exchange SaaS as an internal cryptocurrency processing service for their customers.

Cryptoprocessing entered a global market as CoinsPaid brand in 2018. After a year of rapid growth in the crypto processing business, the ecosystem made a segmentation and dedicated two directions for its business - crypto payment solution for business under Cryptoprocessing brand and a personal wallet for individuals under CoinsPaid brand.
