Invoices

Invoices – a method of bill generation where a given cryptocurrency is tied to a specific fiat amount, which the customer has to pay within a short period of time (15 minutes), after which the invoice has to be regenerated with the new exchange rate

A flexible tool that allows your customers to pay in crypto and credits your account with the required amount in EUR or other currency

Exchange

Cryptoprocessing built-in exchange supports more than 50+ crypto and 15+ fiat currencies. A merchant of our payment gateway can exchange them managing his financial risks.

On-the-fly exchange allows you to accept payments in any supported currency and get the desired one on your balance instantly

OTC desk helps to exchange a large amount of funds at the most preferable rates with a minimum price deviation

Turn Key
